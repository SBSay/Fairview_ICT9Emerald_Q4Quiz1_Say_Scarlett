𝓢𝓲𝓼𝓪
  • Mother of Basilio and Críspin. She is a beautiful woman with perfect brown skin. She is a caring and loving mother, but has a terrible husband who often beats and abuses her and only comes home to get money to gamble and drink. When her two sons went missing, it drives her to the brink of madness. Her sons' deaths take a severe toll on her mental and emotional well-being.
𝓑𝓪𝓼𝓲𝓵𝓲𝓸
  • Críspin's older brother. He works as a sacristan in the church. He earns money by ringing the church bell. After the death of his mother and brother, Sisa and Críspin, he flees the town and is taken in by Kapitan Tiyago and now lives in Manila and becomes a medical student.
𝓒𝓻í𝓼𝓹𝓲𝓷
  • Basilio's younger brother. He works as a sacristan in the church with his brother in San Diego. He earns money by ringing the church bell, but he got accused of stealing 2 gold coins and was abused and killed by the head sacristan and Father Salví.
